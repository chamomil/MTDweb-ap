Privacy Policy: CINEMAGIC CINEMA

This Privacy Policy outlines how CineMagic Cinema collects, uses, and protects personal information obtained from our customers, website visitors, and individuals interacting with our cinema. We will obtain your consent to collect this information and will only use it for the purposes specified.
