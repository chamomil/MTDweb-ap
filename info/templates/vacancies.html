VACANCIES

Customer Service Representative

Description: Provide exceptional customer service by assisting customers with inquiries, resolving issues, and providing information about movie schedules, promotions, and cinema facilities. Maintain a positive and friendly demeanor, handle customer feedback, and ensure customer satisfaction.

Marketing Coordinator

Description: Develop and implement marketing campaigns to promote movies, events, and special screenings. Responsible for social media management, creating engaging content, managing online advertising, and collaborating with external partners to maximize audience reach. Strong communication and marketing skills are required.

Facilities Maintenance Technician

Description: Ensure the smooth operation and maintenance of cinema facilities. Perform routine maintenance and repairs of building systems, including electrical, plumbing, and HVAC. Monitor and address any facility-related issues promptly to maintain a safe and comfortable environment for employees and patrons.

Cleaning Attendant

Description: Responsible for maintaining cleanliness and hygiene throughout the cinema premises, including theater halls, restrooms, lobbies, and common areas. Duties include sweeping, mopping, vacuuming, sanitizing surfaces, emptying trash cans, and restocking supplies as needed. Attention to detail and a diligent work ethic are key to ensure a clean and comfortable environment for cinema patrons and staff.
